These tools (e.g., **treeio**, **ggtree**) address challenges in data parsing, integration and visualization, providing a programmable environment for evolutionary data analysis. ## Biological Knowledge Mining


<table style="border:none; font-size: 90%; width:100%;">
<tr style="border:none;"> 
<td style="border:none;width:25%">

<a href="/contribution-knowledge-mining/"><img src="/images/knowledge-mining/overview.png" style="width:100%; max-width:1000px;"/></a>


</td>
<td style="border:none;">

<a href="/contribution-knowledge-mining/" style="color:black;font-weight:normal">

We have developed methods and tools for biological knowledge mining. Our work—led by **clusterProfiler**—supports functional analysis of multi-omics data through semantic similarity measurement, enrichment analysis, and comparative analysis, extending to single-cell and spatial omics data. These tools assist researchers in interpreting large-scale biological datasets.

</a>
